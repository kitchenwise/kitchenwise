Frequently Asked Questions

Do I really need all 10 items?

No. Start with the thermometer, scale, cast iron skillet, and spatula set. That's $65 and covers 80% of cooking tasks. Add others as budget allows.

Can I buy cheaper versions of these?

For some items, yes. A $5 peeler works fine. But don't cheap out on the thermometer or cast iron — accuracy and durability matter.

What about knives?

A good chef's knife is essential, but it's a separate category. Budget $30-50 for a Victorinox Fibrox 8" chef's knife. It's the best value in kitchen knives.

Should I buy a full cookware set?

No. Sets include pieces you'll never use. Buy individual pans as needed: one non-stick skillet, one cast iron skillet, one saucepan, one stockpot.
