Frequently Asked Questions

Do air fryers actually use less oil?

Yes — 75-90% less than deep frying. Our tests confirmed: fries from the Ninja Air Fryer used 1 tablespoon of oil versus 3 cups for deep frying.

Can I cook frozen food without thawing?

Absolutely. Air fryers excel at frozen food. Frozen fries, nuggets, and wings cook directly from the freezer with better results than oven baking.

What's the best air fryer for beginners?

The Dash Tasti-Crisp — no digital menus, just a dial. If you want digital controls, the Ninja Pro has intuitive buttons and a clear display.
