Electric Kettle FAQ

Is a variable temperature kettle worth it?

If you drink green tea (175°F), oolong (185°F), or French press (200°F), yes. Boiling water (212°F) ruins green tea and makes coffee bitter. For black tea or instant noodles only, a basic boil kettle is fine.

How long do electric kettles last?

3-5 years with daily use. The heating element usually fails first. Cuisinart and Fellow offer 3-year warranties. Hamilton Beach offers 1 year. Descale monthly with vinegar to extend life.

Are plastic kettles safe?

BPA-free plastic is safe but can affect taste. We recommend glass or stainless steel for flavor purity. All kettles we tested use food-grade materials.
