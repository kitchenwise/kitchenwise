Frequently Asked Questions

Can I chop meat in a vegetable chopper?

No. Vegetable choppers aren't designed for meat and can harbor bacteria in crevices. Use a dedicated meat knife or food processor.

Are vegetable choppers dishwasher safe?

Most are top-rack dishwasher safe, but hand-washing preserves blade sharpness longer. Always check the manual.

How often do blades need replacing?

With daily use, blades stay sharp 6-12 months. Replacement blade sets cost $8-15 for most models.

Can I chop nuts in these?

Soft nuts (walnuts, pecans) yes. Hard nuts (almonds, Brazil nuts) will dull blades quickly. Use a dedicated nut chopper instead.
